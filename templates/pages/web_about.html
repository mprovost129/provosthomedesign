About

A business owner building for other business owners.

I am Michael Provost. I built the web platform behind Provost Home Design because the business needed more than a brochure site: it needed plan discovery, inquiry workflows, customer tools, administration, and room to grow.

That experience shapes how I approach web work. The website has to serve the customer, but it also has to make sense for the person maintaining the business after launch. Clear content, reliable forms, useful administration, and honest scope matter more than chasing every trend.

I also built the website for J. Fisk Construction, translating a local contractor's work into a straightforward online presence. Today, the web practice leads with focused websites for local businesses and takes on custom Django applications where the operational need justifies a deeper build.

Working principles

  • Explain the business clearly.
  • Make the next action obvious.
  • Build for mobile and accessibility.
  • Keep ownership and maintenance clear.
  • Use custom code when it creates real value.
