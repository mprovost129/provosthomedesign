Pricing approach

Pricing starts with a defined scope.

A five-page business website and a customer portal should not be priced from the same formula. The proposal connects cost to the work, responsibilities, and outcome.

01 / CONTENT

Pages and readiness

Page count matters, but so do content organization, copy support, photography, and how much existing material can be reused.

02 / FUNCTION

Features and integrations

Forms, payments, accounts, data migration, third-party systems, permissions, and custom workflows change the build and testing effort.

03 / OPERATIONS

Launch and support

Hosting, domain changes, training, maintenance, response expectations, and ongoing improvements are identified rather than assumed.
